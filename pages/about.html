🎯 Our Mission

RevisionHub was created as part of an Extended Project Qualification (EPQ) with a clear goal: to provide free, high-quality revision resources that are properly structured around actual exam board specifications. Too often, students struggle to find organized, reliable study materials that directly align with what they'll actually be tested on.

This website bridges that gap by offering subject-specific resources tailored to AQA, OCR, and Edexcel specifications, with a focus on active recall techniques that have been proven to enhance long-term retention and exam performance.

📖 The EPQ Journey

This website represents months of research, planning, and development as part of my Extended Project Qualification. The EPQ allowed me to explore educational technology, user experience design, and evidence-based learning techniques while creating something genuinely useful for students.

Research & Development

The project involved:

  • Analyzing cognitive science research on effective study techniques
  • Surveying students about their biggest revision challenges
  • Studying exam board specifications across multiple subjects
  • Designing and testing user interfaces for optimal learning
  • Developing a content structure that balances comprehensiveness with accessibility

Key Findings

Through my research, I discovered that:

  • Active recall is significantly more effective than passive re-reading
  • Spaced repetition dramatically improves long-term retention
  • Students need resources organized by specific exam boards, not generic content
  • Minimal, distraction-free design helps maintain focus during study sessions
  • Mobile accessibility is crucial for modern learners
